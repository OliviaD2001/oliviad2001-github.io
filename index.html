About

Currently, a senior majoring in Computer Engineering with a minor in Computer Science at Rutgers New Brunswick, The State University of New Jersey in the School of Engineering.

As an individual who strives to gain knowledge in the software design and hardware development of computer systems, I am planning to pursue this particular field of engineering, with its abundance of opportunities to advance my professional career.

My management and leadership skills are demonstrated through my current position as the Head Lab Supervisor at The Rutgers Office of Information and Technology. Within the department, my position also extends to working with the Help Desk as a Level 2 Consultant and Applications Support Consultant.

In the summer of 2022, I had the opportunity to serve as a student counselor for The Academy at Rutgers for Girls in Engineering and Technology (TARGET). This program aims to increase awareness of and familiarize students with the various career opportunities available in engineering fields. As a counselor, I played a crucial role in helping to facilitate the success of TARGET and its efforts to inspire and educate the next generation of female engineers.
